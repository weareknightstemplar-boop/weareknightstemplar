## DESIGN PRINCIPLES

**CTR-Optimized Formula:**
1. **Curiosity Gap** — Make them need to click
2. **Emotional Trigger** — Fear, hope, anger, curiosity
3. **Clear Value** — They know what they'll get
4. **Visual Contrast** — Stand out in the feed
5. **Face/Expression** — Human connection

**Color Psychology:**
- **Red:** Urgency, passion, danger
- **Gold/Yellow:** Hope, victory, royalty
- **Black:** Power, mystery, seriousness
- **White:** Purity, clarity, truth
- **Blue:** Trust, calm, authority

**Typography:**
- Font: Impact, Bebas Neue, or Oswald (bold, readable)
- Size: Large enough to read on mobile
- Contrast: White text with black outline (or vice versa)
- Max: 3-5 words

## SHORTS THUMBNAILS (YouTube Shorts)

Shorts thumbnails are less important (title matters more), but here are key principles:

1. **First Frame Matters:** The first second is your thumbnail
2. **Text Overlay:** Use large, readable text on video
3. **Hook in 1 Second:** Show the most interesting frame first
4. **Vertical Format:** 9:16 ratio

**Shorts Text Overlay Formulas:**
- "3 things that..."
- "Stop doing this..."
- "I wish I knew..."
- "This changed everything..."
- "Most people don't know..."

## A/B TESTING STRATEGY

**Test These Variables:**
1. **Your Expression:** Serious vs. smiling vs. shocked
2. **Text Placement:** Top vs. bottom vs. center
3. **Background:** Dark vs. light vs. colorful
4. **Text Amount:** 3 words vs. 5 words
5. **Color Scheme:** Red vs. blue vs. gold

**How to Test:**
1. Create 2 versions of same thumbnail
2. Use YouTube's Test & Compare feature
3. Run for 1-2 weeks
4. Keep the winner, iterate
