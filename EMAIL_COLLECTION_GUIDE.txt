YOUR GOAL: Get visitors to enter their email address

================================================================================
STEP 1: CREATE THE FORM IN CONVERTKIT
================================================================================

1. Log into ConvertKit
2. Click "Grow" (top menu)
3. Click "Landing Pages & Forms"
4. Click "+ Create New"
5. Select "Form"
6. Choose template: "Simple" or "Minimal"
7. Edit the form:

   HEADLINE: 
   "Get 7 Free Prayers for the Modern Warrior"

   SUBHEADLINE:
   "Join 1,000+ Christian men fighting the good fight"

   BUTTON TEXT:
   "Send Me the Prayers"

   SUCCESS MESSAGE:
   "Check your email! The prayers are on their way."

8. Click "Save"

================================================================================
STEP 2: CONNECT TO YOUR EMAIL SEQUENCE
================================================================================

1. In your form settings, click "Settings" tab
2. Under "Sequence", select:
   "Welcome Sequence - Warrior Challenge"
3. This means: When someone subscribes, they get the 7 emails automatically
4. Click "Save"

================================================================================
STEP 3: GET THE EMBED CODE
================================================================================

1. Click "Embed" tab in your form
2. Select "HTML" (not JavaScript or WordPress)
3. Click "Copy Code"
4. The code looks like this:

   <script async data-uid="YOUR-UID-HERE" 
   src="https://YOUR-URL.ck.page/YOUR-UID/index.js"></script>

================================================================================
STEP 4: ADD TO YOUR LANDING PAGE
================================================================================

1. Open index.html in any text editor
   (Notepad, TextEdit, VS Code, etc.)

2. Find this line (around line 108):

   </section>

   (This is the closing tag of the hero section)

3. PASTE this code RIGHT AFTER that line:

   <!-- EMAIL SIGNUP SECTION -->
   <section style="padding: 4rem 2rem; background: #1a1510; text-align: center;">
       <div style="max-width: 500px; margin: 0 auto;">
           <h2 style="font-size: 1.75rem; color: #D4AF37; margin-bottom: 1rem;">
               Get 7 Free Prayers for the Modern Warrior
           </h2>
           <p style="color: #888; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
               Join 1,000+ Christian men fighting the good fight
           </p>

           [PASTE YOUR CONVERTKIT CODE HERE]

           <p style="color: #666; font-size: 0.75rem; margin-top: 1.5rem;">
               🔒 We respect your privacy. Unsubscribe anytime.
           </p>
       </div>
   </section>

4. Replace [PASTE YOUR CONVERTKIT CODE HERE] with the code from Step 3

5. Save the file

================================================================================
STEP 5: UPLOAD TO NETLIFY
================================================================================

1. Go to netlify.com
2. Drag & drop your updated folder
3. Your landing page now has an email signup form!

🎯 OPTIMIZATION TIPS
================================================================================

✅ PLACE THE FORM ABOVE THE FOLD

================================================================================
❓ TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

Q: Form not showing up?
A: Make sure you pasted the ConvertKit code correctly (not the snippet I gave you,
   but your ACTUAL code from ConvertKit)

Q: Emails not sending?
A: Check ConvertKit → Automations. Make sure the sequence is published and
   connected to the form

Q: People subscribing but not getting emails?
A: Check the "Sequence" setting in your form. It should say:
   "Welcome Sequence - Warrior Challenge"

Q: How do I see who subscribed?
A: ConvertKit → Subscribers. You'll see everyone who signed up
