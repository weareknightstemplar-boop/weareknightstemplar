## ⚙️ Step-by-Step ConvertKit Setup

### Step 1: Verify Your Form

1. Go to **convertkit.com** → Sign in
2. Click **"Forms & Landing Pages"** in the menu
3. Find your form: **"7 Prayers for the Modern Warrior"**
4. Click on it to verify it's active
5. Note the Form ID: `54448a7d37` ✓

---

### Step 2: Create the Automation

1. Click **"Automations"** in the top menu
2. Click **"Create Automation"** (blue button)
3. Choose **"Create from scratch"**
4. Name it: **"7 Prayers Funnel → 30-Day Challenge"**

---

### Step 3: Set the Trigger

1. Click **"Add Event"**
2. Select: **"Subscribes to a form"**
3. Choose your form: **"7 Prayers for the Modern Warrior"**
4. Click **"Add Event"**

### Step 5: Add the PDF Download

**Option A: Host PDF on ConvertKit**
1. Go to **"Broadcasts"** → **"Assets"**
2. Upload your `7-Prayers-for-the-Modern-Warrior-FREE.pdf`
3. Copy the download link
4. Paste in Email #1

**Option B: Host PDF on Gumroad (Recommended)**
1. In Gumroad, set your "7 Days of Prayer" product to $0
2. Make it "Pay what you want" with $0 minimum

1. Click **"Preview"** in the automation

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Emails not sending"
- Check automation is ACTIVE (not paused)
- Verify subscriber is tagged correctly
- Check spam folder

### "PDF link not working"
- Test the link in incognito mode
- Make sure Gumroad product is published (not draft)

### "Form not showing on website"
- Hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R
- Check browser console for errors
- Verify form ID: `54448a7d37`
