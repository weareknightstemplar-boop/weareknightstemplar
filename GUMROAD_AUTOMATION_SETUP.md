## 🚀 Step-by-Step Setup

### STEP 1: Create Your Free Product on Gumroad

1. Go to **gumroad.com** → **Products** → **"New Product"**
2. Select **"Digital Product"**
3. Name: **"7 Days of Prayer for the Modern Warrior"**
4. Set price to **$0** (or "Pay what you want" with $0 minimum)
5. Upload your PDF: `7-Prayers-for-the-Modern-Warrior-FREE.pdf`
6. Click **"Publish"**
7. Copy the product URL (will be: `brothermark.gumroad.com/l/XXXXX`)

---

### STEP 2: Set Up the 7-Day Email Workflow

1. In Gumroad, go to **"Workflows"** in the left menu
2. Click **"New Workflow"**
3. Name it: **"7 Prayers Funnel → 30-Day Challenge"**
4. Trigger: Select **"When someone buys"** → Choose your "7 Days of Prayer" product
5. Click **"Create Workflow"**

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### "Workflow not sending emails"
- Check workflow is ACTIVE (not paused)
- Verify trigger product is correct
- Test with your own email

### "Emails going to spam"
- Add SPF/DKIM records (Gumroad will guide you)
- Keep subject lines clean (no ALL CAPS)
- Avoid spam words: "FREE", "URGENT", "ACT NOW"

### "Low open rates"
- Test different subject lines
- Send at optimal times (Tuesday-Thursday, 10am or 2pm)
- Keep emails short and personal
