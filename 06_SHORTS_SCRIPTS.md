## FILMING TIPS FOR SHORTS

**Technical:**
- Film in 9:16 (vertical)
- 1080x1920 resolution minimum
- Good lighting (ring light or natural window)
- Clear audio (use external mic if possible)

**Performance:**
- Energy level: 150% of normal
- First 1 second is everything
- Look directly at camera
- Use hand gestures
- Show emotion

**Editing:**
- Quick cuts (every 2-3 seconds)
- Text overlays for key points
- Background music (trending if possible)
- End with clear CTA

**Posting:**
- Post daily for algorithm boost
- Best times: 8-10 AM, 12-2 PM, 6-8 PM
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Respond to comments quickly
